ClojureDays 2020 has been cancelled

Thank your for your enthousiastic reaction to this year's edition of Dutch Clojure Days. We receieved a fantastic response in both our CFP and ticket sale and would have loved to organize yet another great programme.

Unfortunately the Corona crisis makes it impossible this year to organise the planned event safely. That's why we've decided to cancel this year's ClojureDays conference.

We are considering options for organising another kind of event at a later time. Stay tuned for news and make sure to follow us on twitter.

On behalf of the Dutch Clojure Days team
Carlo Sciolla
